How to Calculate GPA at COMSATS?

At COMSATS University Islamabad, GPA is calculated using a weighted average formula based on credit hours and grade points.

GPA Formula: GPA = Σ (Credit Hours * Grade Points) / Σ Credit Hours

Each subject contributes according to its credit weight. Higher credit courses impact your GPA more significantly.

FAQ's

How is GPA calculated at COMSATS University Islamabad?

The GPA at COMSATS University Islamabad is calculated using a weighted average formula based on credit hours and grade points earned in each course.

What is the official COMSATS grading scale for GPA calculation?

COMSATS follows a 4.00 GPA scale where each letter grade corresponds to specific grade points used to compute SGPA and CGPA.

How do I calculate SGPA for a single semester at COMSATS?

To calculate Semester GPA (SGPA), multiply each course’s credit hours by its grade points, add them together, and divide by the total semester credit hours.

How is CGPA calculated at COMSATS?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is calculated by combining all completed semesters and dividing total earned grade points by total attempted credit hours.

Does this COMSATS GPA calculator support both SGPA and CGPA?

Yes, this tool calculates both Semester GPA and Cumulative GPA according to the official COMSATS academic policy.

Can I calculate GPA using credit hours from different semesters?

For CGPA calculation, you must include all previous semesters' credit hours and grade points to get an accurate cumulative result.

What happens if I repeat a course at COMSATS?
