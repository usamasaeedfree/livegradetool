About This UAF CGPA Calculator

This calculator is designed for students of the University of Agriculture Faisalabad (UAF) to accurately calculate semester and cumulative CGPA based on the official grading system.

The calculations follow the standard credit-hour weighted formula used by universities: CGPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credit Hours.

Step-by-Step Guide to Calculate Your CGPA

  1. Enter the subjects you have taken in each semester.
  2. Enter credit hours for each subject.
  3. Select the grade you received.
  4. Add multiple semesters if needed using the "+ Add Semester" button.
  5. Your semester GPA and cumulative CGPA will automatically calculate.
  6. Copy or download your results for future reference.

Example: If you scored 'A' in a 3-credit course, grade points = 4 × 3 = 12. Sum all grade points and divide by total credit hours for CGPA.

UAF Grading System Explained

Grades at UAF are assigned based on your marks in each subject. Each grade corresponds to a grade point as follows:

Marks (%) Grade Grade Points
90–100 A 4.00
85–89 A- 3.67
80–84 B+ 3.33
75–79 B 3.00
70–74 C+ 2.33
65–69 C 2.00
50–64 D 1.00
0–49 F 0.00

Semester GPA vs Cumulative GPA

Semester GPA: The GPA calculated for a single semester based on courses taken in that semester.

Cumulative GPA (CGPA): The average of all semester GPAs across your degree program, weighted by credit hours.

Use this calculator to track both semester GPA and CGPA for better academic planning.

UAF Academic CGPA Policies

  • Minimum CGPA to pass a semester: 2.00
  • Minimum cumulative CGPA to graduate: 2.50
  • Scholarship eligibility may require CGPA ≥ 3.50
  • Repeated courses will replace the previous grade in CGPA calculation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I calculate my CGPA if I repeated a course?

Only the latest attempt is counted in the CGPA calculation at UAF.

Can I include elective courses in my CGPA calculation?

Yes, all courses with credit hours included in your transcript count toward your CGPA.

How does UAF round CGPA?

CGPA is rounded to two decimal places.

What is the difference between GPA and CGPA at UAF?

GPA is for one semester; CGPA is cumulative across all semesters.

Is this calculator officially affiliated with UAF?

No. This is an educational tool developed independently to help students.

Can I export my CGPA calculation to PDF?

Yes, use the "Download Result" button to save a report.

How can I improve my CGPA in upcoming semesters?

Focus on higher credit courses and aim for better grades in each subject.
