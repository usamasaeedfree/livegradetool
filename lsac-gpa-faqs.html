What is an LSAC GPA?

The LSAC GPA is a standardized GPA recalculated using the LSAC CAS 4.33 grading scale. It is calculated by converting all your undergraduate grades from every institution you attended into a uniform scale. Law schools use this GPA to compare applicants consistently, regardless of the grading systems used at different schools.
